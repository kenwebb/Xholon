Xholon Twine

Xholon apps can be exported as Twine 2 interactive stories. The examples included on this page are a few initial tests. They demonstrate that the structures in Xholon and Twine are compatible with each other. There's still a lot of work to do.

Hello World is a very simple example.

Cell simplistically models cell metabolism.

Pride and Prejudice captures relationships between some of the characters in Jane Austen's novel.
